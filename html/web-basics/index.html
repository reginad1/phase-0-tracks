Who run the world? Cats

My love for cats is one that began relatively recently. I grew up with dogs and didn't know my affinity for the feline friend until a few years ago.

Reasons Cats Rock

  • They are incredibly intelligent
  • They sleep on your pillow at night
  • They greet you at the door when they get home
  • They help relieve stress

At this point in my life, I'm comfortable calling myself a cat person. I have two very large tabby cats. One orange one, and one gray one.
