Summer Veggies

What summer is all about..

"In the spring at the end of the day, you should smell like dirt" -Margaret Atwood
  1. Cauliflower

    2. Cauliflower is a fantastic summer vegetable. It is great in stir frys, in smoothies, and can even be used a gluten free substitute for pizza crust.

  2. Tomatoes

    3. Tomatoes are the red shining star of every summer. Tomato sandwiches with homemade mayo, a little salt and pepper are the best thing ever. Use tomatoes in salads, or make a big batch of tomato sauce to save for pasta and pizza later on!

  3. Cucumbers

    4. Cucumbers fresh from the garden are one of the most delectable things a person can eat on a hot summer day. Toss some slices into a jug of ice water for a refreshing pick-me-up in the afternoon, or along side some tomatoes and feta for a fresh snack or side dish.
